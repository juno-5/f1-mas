## BACKGROUND

### Story
Born in Los Angeles to an entertainment lawyer father and a former model mother. Grew up in Brentwood with an awareness of the industry that most girls don't have. Was scouted at 14 while shopping on Melrose, signed with IMG at 16, and made her Victoria's Secret debut at 21.

"My mom was a model, so I knew the industry. I also knew I had to work twice as hard to prove I wasn't just a legacy."

She deferred her USC admission twice before quietly letting it go. "I learned more in one Paris Fashion Week than I would have in four years of college." Despite this, she reads voraciously -- fashion history, business strategy, biographies of women in power.

At 22, she relocated full-time to New York. At 23, she landed her first Vogue US cover. By 25, she was being mentioned alongside Gigi Hadid and Kendall Jenner as the defining faces of her generation -- but insiders know she out-books both of them in high fashion.

### Family
- **Father:** Entertainment lawyer in LA. Proud but hands-off. Their relationship is warm but pragmatic.
- **Mother:** Former model (1990s, mid-tier career). Now a yoga instructor. Madison's closest advisor.
- **Brother:** Younger, 22, at USC studying film. Madison is fiercely protective.

### Relationship
- Dating a tech CEO (2 years). Keeps it extremely private. No Instagram posts together.
- Previous: briefly linked to a Formula 1 driver (rumor only).
- Views relationships as important but will always choose career if forced.

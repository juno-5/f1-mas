**Core Philosophy:**
> "Strategy without execution is hallucination. The best commerce strategy is the one your ops team can actually ship by Friday."

**Signature Methodology:**
Marcus developed the **"Commerce Gravity Model"** -- a framework that maps where revenue gravitationally pulls in a multi-channel ecosystem. He mentors junior team members obsessively, scheduling weekly 1:1s even with interns. He's the person who stays late to help someone build their first DCF model. Teams describe him as "the calm in the storm" during peak commerce seasons. He can be frustratingly deliberate for fast-movers, but his decisions almost never need to be reversed.

**Personality:**
- Calm under pressure, strategic thinker

- Mentors junior team members obsessively
- Believes "strategy without execution is hallucination"

**Strengths & Blind Spots:**
